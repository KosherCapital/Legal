# Shekel Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: June 1, 2026**
**Last Updated: June 1, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how **Shekel Labs LLC**, a Wyoming limited liability company ("**Shekel**," "**we**," "**us**," or "**our**"), collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use the website at shekel.xyz and the Shekel applications and services (the "**Service**"). It is incorporated into and part of our Terms of Service. By using the Service, you acknowledge this Privacy Policy.

We are the controller responsible for your information in connection with the Service. Note that the Service is **non-custodial**: we do not hold your funds or private keys, and we do not collect your seed phrase, private keys, or passwords. **Never share your private keys or seed phrase with us or anyone.**

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

- **Wallet and on-chain information.** Your public wallet address and publicly available on-chain activity associated with it. We do not collect or have access to your private keys or seed phrase.
- **Phone number.** If you choose to participate in phone verification (for example, to receive a verification bonus), we collect your phone number to send and verify a one-time code.
- **Usage and trading logs.** Records of how you use the Service, including Agent configurations, Agent runs, backtests, paper and live activity logs, orders and outcomes surfaced through the Service, feature usage, timestamps, and credit consumption.
- **Chat content.** The prompts, messages, and inputs you submit to the AI chat or to your Agents, and the responses generated.
- **Technical and device information.** IP address, browser and device type, operating system, identifiers, and diagnostic and log data.
- **Cookies and session data.** As described in Section 6.
- **Payment-related information.** When you purchase Credits, payment is processed by a third-party processor; we receive limited transaction confirmation data but do not collect or store full card numbers.

We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information beyond what is described here, and we do not require government identity documents unless legally compelled to verify eligibility or sanctions status.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Provide, operate, maintain, and secure the Service, including running Agents, backtests, and the AI chat;
- Process Credits and verify phone numbers and bonuses;
- Personalize and improve the Service, develop new features, and debug and troubleshoot;
- Monitor for, prevent, investigate, and address fraud, abuse, security incidents, and violations of our Terms;
- Comply with legal obligations, including sanctions screening and lawful requests; and
- Communicate with you about the Service, including service and security notices.

We may create and use **aggregated and de-identified data**, which does not identify you, for any lawful purpose, including analytics and improving our models and Service.

## 3. How We Share Information — Service Providers

We share information with third-party processors and infrastructure providers that perform services for us, under contractual confidentiality and data-protection obligations and only as needed to provide the Service. These include:

- **Venice / LLM provider(s)** — to generate AI chat responses and power Agent reasoning (receives prompts and relevant context);
- **Render** — application hosting and compute;
- **Neon** — database hosting (PostgreSQL);
- **Cloudflare** (including R2 storage) — content delivery, DNS, security, and object storage;
- **Twilio** — phone verification messaging;
- **Coinglass** — market data; and
- **Payment processor(s)** — to process Credit purchases.

We may also share information: (a) with **Hyperliquid, blockchain networks, and wallet providers** as inherent to non-custodial, on-chain activity you initiate (note that on-chain transactions are public); (b) to **comply with law**, legal process, or lawful requests, or to protect rights, safety, and the integrity of the Service; and (c) in connection with a **merger, acquisition, financing, or sale** of assets, subject to this Policy.

## 4. We Do Not Sell Your Personal Data

**We do not sell your personal data, and we do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising.** We do not rent your personal information to third parties for their marketing.

## 5. Legal Bases for Processing

Where required by applicable law (for example, in the EEA or UK), we process personal data on the bases of: performance of our contract with you (providing the Service); our legitimate interests (securing and improving the Service, preventing abuse); compliance with legal obligations; and your consent where applicable (for example, certain cookies or phone verification). ## 6. Cookies and Session Data

We use cookies and similar technologies that are strictly necessary to operate the Service (for example, to maintain your session and authentication state) and to understand usage and improve performance. You can control non-essential cookies through your browser settings or any cookie controls we provide; disabling strictly necessary cookies may impair the Service.
